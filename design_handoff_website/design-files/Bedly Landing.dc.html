How it works

Four taps to a tidier you

No spreadsheets, no guilt trips. Just a quick morning ritual that earns you the rest of your day.

1

Scan your bed

Point your camera at your freshly-made bed. BedBuddy's AI checks the pillows, corners and fluff in seconds.

2

Get your score

A 0–100 rating lands instantly, with friendly tips to level up tomorrow. Hospital corners? Chef's kiss.

3

Unlock your day

Keep your most distracting apps — Instagram, TikTok, X — locked until your bed is made. Make it to earn your morning scroll.

4

Build your streak

Rack up points and climb your squad's leaderboard. Nudge the friends who are slacking. Lovingly.
